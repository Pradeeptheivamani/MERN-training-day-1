MY BIO DATA

Meaning & Origin: The name is derived from the Sanskrit word pradīpa, where "Pra" means "forth/forward" and "deep" means "lamp/light". It literally translates to "Light," "Lantern," or "One who illuminates". Symbolism: In Indian traditions, it symbolizes knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment. It is associated with the Diya (lamp) used in religious ceremonies (Puja) to dispel darkness and ignorance.
